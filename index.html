Welcome to Meme Forecast

This application is created to forecast memes. If you place your mouse(computer) or click(mobile) on a forecast box, you will see the meme. The meme will be chosen depending on that location's weather. You can can see all memes currently matched to available weather in the first forecast box. You can also see the weather and meme for any city in the OpenWeather API. Have fun!
